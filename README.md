# Sales-Performance-Analysis-Dashboard
This project visualizes the overall sales performance of a company across different years, regions, sales representatives, and products. It helps management understand key metrics such as total sales, total units sold, top-performing sales reps and products, as well as yearly and regional trends.
The dashboard provides actionable insights for improving product focus, regional strategy, and sales rep performance.

Key Objectives

1)To monitor company-wide sales performance through interactive KPIs and charts.
2)To identify top-performing products and sales representatives.
3)To analyze year-wise and region-wise sales trends.
4)To compare sales performance across different months, years, and regions.
5)To identify low-performing products for strategic improvement.

some Questions which are solved in this project

1)Which sales representatives generated the highest sales?	
2)Which are the top 5 products based on total sales value?	
3)How did the sales vary year-wise?
4)How did the number of units sold vary year-wise?	
5)Which are the top 5 months with the highest sales in each year?	
6)Which top 5 products sold in every region?	
7)Which bottom 5 products have the lowest sales across all regions?
